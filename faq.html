At PlayOne, we implement KYC to protect your account from fraud, prevent identity theft, and ensure compliance with Belgian gaming laws. This process confirms that you are of legal gambling age and helps maintain a safe gaming environment for all players.

What documents do I need for account verification at PlayOne?

To complete your verification at PlayOne, you'll typically need to provide:

PlayOne strives to complete account verification within 24-48 hours of receiving all required documents. During peak periods, this may extend to 72 hours.
